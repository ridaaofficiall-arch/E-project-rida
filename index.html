About Nail Care

Healthy nails are an essential part of personal grooming and beauty. Proper nail care helps prevent breakage, dryness, infections, and keeps your hands looking elegant and well-maintained. With the right routine and professional treatments, you can achieve strong, shiny, and beautiful nails.

  • Keep your nails clean and dry to avoid infections.
  • Moisturize your hands and cuticles daily.
  • Trim your nails regularly to maintain a healthy shape.
  • Avoid biting your nails or picking at cuticles.
  • Use gentle, acetone-free nail polish removers.
  • Give your nails a break from polish to let them breathe.
  • Wear gloves while doing household chores to prevent damage.
